# ClawMore Commercial Success Metrics & Quality Targets

## Executive Summary

This document defines the comprehensive metrics framework and quality targets required for ClawMore to achieve commercial success. These metrics align with the Evolution-as-a-Service (EaaS) business model and provide clear targets for each phase of growth.

## Appendix: Metric Calculation Formulas

### Financial Formulas

- **MRR** = Sum of all monthly subscription revenue
- **ARPU** = MRR / Number of active customers
- **LTV** = ARPU × Average customer lifetime (months)
- **CAC** = Total sales & marketing spend / New customers acquired
- **Gross Margin** = (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100%

### Customer Formulas

- **Churn Rate** = (Customers lost during period / Customers at start) × 100%
- **Retention Rate** = 100% - Churn Rate
- **NPS** = % Promoters - % Detractors (from survey)
- **Activation Rate** = (Activated users / Total signups) × 100%

### Product Formulas

- **Mutation Success Rate** = (Successful mutations / Total mutations) × 100%
- **Evolution Adoption** = (Clients with evolution / Total clients) × 100%
- **Context Efficiency** = (Original tokens - Optimized tokens) / Original tokens × 100%
